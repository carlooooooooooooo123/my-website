About Cebu City

Welcome to Cebu City, the "Queen City of the South." Here, adventure, culture, and breathtaking landscapes come together. From iconic historical landmarks to pristine white-sand beaches, Cebu is waiting to share its beauty with you!

Our Services

  • Friendly guided city and island tours with locals
  • Comfortable hotel and resort bookings
  • Transportation and travel assistance—We’ve got you covered!
  • Event schedules—Don't miss out on the best festivals!

Top Attractions

Our Gorgeous Beaches

Feel the sand between your toes and the sun on your skin at Mactan, Bantayan, and Malapascua. These beaches are as friendly as our people!

Historical Landmarks

Step back in time and discover our heritage at Magellan's Cross, Fort San Pedro, and the Basilica Minore del Santo Niño.

Adventure Awaits

Looking for thrill? Go canyoneering at Kawasan Falls or swim with whale sharks in Oslob. It’s an experience you won’t forget!
